About the Resort

America's Largest Ski Resort

Park City Mountain is Utah's premier snowboarding and skiing destination, located just 30 minutes from Salt Lake City and its international airport. Situated at an elevation of 10,026 feet at its summit, the resort receives an average of 355 inches of legendary Utah powder each season. Since its merger with Canyons Resort in 2015, Park City Mountain became the largest ski resort in the United States — offering over 330 trails, 41 lifts, and something for every ability level, from beginner groomers to expert-only chutes and world-class terrain parks.
